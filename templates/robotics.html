ROBOTICS AND AUTOMATION

What is Robotics?

Robotics combines engineering, computer science, and technology to create machines capable of performing tasks traditionally requiring human intelligence and dexterity. Robots have revolutionized industries by enhancing accuracy, consistency, and speed.

What is Automation?

Automation is the use of control systems, such as computers or robots, to manage industrial processes, thus reducing the need for human intervention. It enhances productivity and reduces operational costs.

Applications in Electrical and Electronics

Robotics and automation play crucial roles in the electrical and electronics sector:

  • Manufacturing: Automated assembly lines are used to build electronic components with precision and minimal error.
  • Inspection: Robots equipped with sensors are used for quality assurance and defect detection in electronics.
  • Renewable Energy: Automation is employed in solar panel manufacturing and maintenance of wind turbines.
  • Power Systems: Robotics help in maintaining high-voltage power lines and substations, ensuring safety and efficiency.
  • IoT Integration: Smart systems powered by automation optimize energy usage and monitor electrical grids in real-time.
